Product Description

This portable Bluetooth speaker delivers exceptional sound quality that defies its compact size. Engineered with advanced audio technology and built for adventure, it's the perfect companion for any lifestyle.

Superior Audio Performance

The 360-degree sound technology creates an immersive listening experience that fills any space with rich, detailed audio. Dual high-performance drivers and passive radiators deliver deep bass, clear mids, and crisp highs across the entire frequency spectrum.

Built for Adventure

With an IPX7 waterproof rating, this speaker can handle splashes, rain, and even full submersion up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. Whether you're at the beach, by the pool, or caught in a downpour, your music keeps playing.

All-Day Power

The high-capacity battery provides up to 20 hours of continuous playback on a single charge. When your devices need power, the speaker doubles as a power bank to charge your smartphone or other USB devices.

Easy Connectivity

Bluetooth 5.0 technology ensures stable connectivity up to 100 feet away. Pair two speakers together using True Wireless Stereo for an even more immersive sound experience that fills larger spaces.

Hands-Free Convenience

The built-in microphone enables crystal-clear hands-free calling. Voice assistant compatibility lets you control your music and smart home devices with simple voice commands.
